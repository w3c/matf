## Success Criterion 2.4.2 Page Titled

(Level A)

[wcag:page-titled]
[wcag2ict:applying-sc-2-4-2-page-titled-to-non-web-software]

This applies directly as written, and as described in Intent from Understanding Success Criterion 2.4.2, replacing "Web pages" with "Pages".

With this substitution, it would read:

**2.4.2 Page Titled:** Pages have titles that describe topic or purpose.

[note:A page title should be provided through visible text. It must be specific to each page and not reused across distinct pages in a way that makes the user's location or purpose unclear.]

[example:Examples of a page title include: (1) a title in a navigation bar; (2) a heading at the top of the page; (3) a highlighted tab in a tab bar.]
